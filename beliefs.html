Beliefs

What does leadership mean to me?

Leadership to me means to be able to work calmly under stressful situations. Leadership means to be able to work well with others and grasp concepts and do everything in your power to get the most desirable outcome. Leadership means to be a good role model and being able to take charge.

What does teamwork mean to me?

Teamwork to me means working with people around you to build on eachothers ideas to come to the best possible solution. Working together and helping eachother.
